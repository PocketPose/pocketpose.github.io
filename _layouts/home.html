Knowledge Hub: Learn Human Pose Estimation

Whether you're new to Human Pose Estimation (HPE) or an experienced researcher, our Knowledge Hub is designed to help you explore and deepen your understanding of HPE concepts, methods, and applications.

We provide structured learning resources at all levels, from foundational concepts to advanced techniques. Start with the Basic Concepts to grasp core principles, then explore topics like deep learning-based pose estimation, 3D human pose recovery, multi-person tracking, and real-time implementation.
